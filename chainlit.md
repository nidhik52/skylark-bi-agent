# Skylark BI Agent

Live Monday.com data, answered in plain English.

---

### What you can ask

**Pipeline**
- How is our pipeline looking right now?
- What is the total deal value this quarter?
- Break down pipeline by sector

**Risks**
- Which deals are at highest risk?
- What deals have overdue close dates?
- What work orders are overdue?

**Revenue & Billing**
- Show me our total accounts receivable
- What is our unbilled amount across all work orders?
- Break down revenue by sector

**Search**
- Show open deals in Powerline with high probability
- Find all deals owned by [owner code]

---

### How it works

Every answer pulls **live data** directly from your Monday.com boards via MCP tools.
No cached results. Numbers are always current.
